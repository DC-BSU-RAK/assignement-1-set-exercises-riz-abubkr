Overview

Genre: Romance,Drama

Plot Overview"When life gives you Tangerine"In Jeju, a spirited girl and a steadfast boy's island story blossoms into a lifelong tale of setbacks and triumphs-proving love endures across time.

Characters

  • Oh Ae-Sun-Played by IU, she is a spirited and rebellious young woman with a love for books, dreaming of becoming a poet despite her challenging circumstances.
  • Yang Gwan-Sik-Portrayed by Park Bo-Gum, he is a sincere and diligent man who deeply loves Ae-Sun.
  • Middle-aged Ae-Sun and Gwan-Sik--Played by Moon So-Ri and Park Hae-Joon, respectively, showcasing their journey through life.
